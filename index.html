About Us

Aether is popularly believed to be the substance that permeates everything between spaces, a singularity which predates the universe and reality. Why Aether?

To hear one demands silence. We build houses but it's the space inside that makes it livable. We shape clay to pots but it's the emptiness inside that holds.

Aether, that which fills everything is the spirit of the annual techno-cultural fest of Mahindra École Centrale. Let us together embark on a journey towards discovering Aether, the space between transcendence and euphoria!

Events

Aether has always promised a consortium of cultural, technical, and literary events that will span over a period of two days, aimed at bringing out the all-round talents of individuals. From events that wrack your brains like MECHacks, to completely fun-filled games like Filmy Fungama, Aether has something for everyone. Our pro-shows have constantly been the soul of the fest featuring renowned celebrities from all over the country.


Cultural

From film and pop culture trivia to mind boggling quizzes, these events are all about having fun with your team while getting to the top!

Technical

Hackathons and plane building; it's all about being hands on and using your technical skills to race to the finish!

Register

For entry to the fest cultural events, the day pass is necessary. A QR code will be generated which you will be required to show at the gate on entrance and will be given a band.

Please ensure the size of your team is not more than the maximum given by the event rules. For team events such as Groove, MasterShot, MECHacks, etc, only one person will be able to register per team. Under the attendee name slot, please enter your teammates names as well.

College ID is compulsory for entry at the gate.

MEC students are not required to take day passes nor do they have to register for MECHacks and Groove (They do however need to register for every other event that requires a fee)
